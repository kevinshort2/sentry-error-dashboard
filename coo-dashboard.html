The 5 Pillars of COO Excellence

1. Strategic Execution

  • Transform strategy into actionable plans
  • Bridge the gap between vision and reality
  • Own quarterly OKRs and cascade them
  • Create accountability frameworks
  • Review progress weekly, not monthly

2. Operational Mastery

  • Build scalable systems, not one-off solutions
  • Document processes ruthlessly
  • Automate repetitive tasks immediately
  • Track leading indicators, not just results
  • Cut waste without cutting value

3. Team Amplification

  • Hire people better than yourself
  • Delegate outcomes, not tasks
  • Give feedback fast and direct
  • Remove blockers before asked

4. Financial Discipline

  • Know your unit economics cold

  • Invest in force multipliers
  • Tie spending to measurable outcomes

5. Cross-Functional Leadership

  • Break down departmental silos
  • Speak every team's language
  • Resolve conflicts with data, not politics
  • Align incentives across functions
  • Build trust through transparency
