30‑Day Action Plan

  1. Implement Structured Decision‑Making – Adopt a lightweight framework (RAPID or DACI) and document owner, criteria, timeline for each key choice.
  2. Clarify Task Ownership & Deadlines – When assigning work, specify responsible person, expected completion date, and measurable outcome; track in a tool.
  3. Standardize Meeting Cadence & Agendas – Create a template with purpose, agenda items, owners, and desired decisions; circulate 15 min before each meeting.
  4. Encourage Structured Feedback Loops – Hold brief post‑mortem or retro sessions after major incidents/releases to capture lessons learned.
  5. Balance Technical Focus with Strategic Planning – Set a recurring bi‑weekly strategic review meeting to discuss roadmap, priorities, and long‑term metrics.

Weekly Reflection Questions

  1. What progress have I made toward formalizing decision‑making this week?
  2. How clearly did I define task owners and deadlines for delegated work?
  3. Did my meetings have a pre‑set agenda and clear outcomes? What can be improved?
  4. What feedback loops did I introduce, and what insights emerged?
  5. How did I allocate time for strategic planning versus immediate technical issues?
